There are mainly three types of brakes: Rim brakes, caliper brakes, and drum brakes. Each type of brakes have their own unique mechanism but all have one goal, to reduce or even to completely stop the acceleration of the bicycle. A good bike should have a front and a rear brake to ensure safety when cycling specially in a very busy road. It is not easy to tell, specially for those who are new to bicycles, what kind of brakes does a bicycle have and also how good the quality is.
